About

I've been building worlds since college — not just as projects, but as experiments in systems, emotion, and possibility. From lines of C++ to living simulations, I craft mechanics that breathe, AI that thinks, and spaces that invite players to lose themselves.

I am a game developer drawn to depth — to systems that interact, react, and evolve. Every project is a new universe, engineered with precision and built with the curiosity of someone who still believes games can change the way we see the world.
